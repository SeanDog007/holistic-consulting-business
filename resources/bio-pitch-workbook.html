Elevator Pitch

A natural-sounding 30-second introduction. Not a sales pitch — a clear, confident answer to “So what do you do?”

HC Pitch Formula

“I help [audience] who struggle with [problem] to [outcome] through [approach]—without [common fear].”

Practice Tip

Record yourself saying this at least 3 times. Listen back. If anything sounds scripted, rewrite it in the way you’d say it to a friend at a coffee shop.

Example 1
NTP — Digestive Health Focus
Long Bio

I’m a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner (NTP) certified through the Nutritional Therapy Association, specializing in digestive health for adults who’ve been told “just eat more fiber” one too many times. My clients are women and men ages 25–55 dealing with chronic bloating, food sensitivities, and unpredictable digestion that’s taken over their daily life. Through personalized nutrition assessments, targeted elimination protocols, and whole-food wellness plans built around your actual life (not a textbook), I help clients understand what’s driving their symptoms and build eating habits that make their gut feel normal again. My approach is bio-individual and food-first — because there’s no one-size-fits-all fix for digestion. Before becoming an NTP, I spent years managing my own chronic digestive issues through nutrition, which is exactly why I do this work. I see clients virtually and in person in Portland, Oregon.

Short Bio

I’m an NTP specializing in digestive health for adults dealing with chronic bloating, food sensitivities, and unpredictable digestion. Through personalized assessments and whole-food protocols, I help clients understand their symptoms and build habits that make their gut feel normal again. Bio-individual, food-first, and built around real life.

Micro Bio

NTP helping adults with chronic digestive issues find relief through personalized, whole-food nutrition.

Example 2
IIN Health Coach — Women’s Wellness
Long Bio

I’m a certified Integrative Nutrition Health Coach helping busy professional women ages 30–50 who are exhausted by diet culture and ready for an approach that actually fits their life. My clients are smart, ambitious women juggling careers, families, and their own wellbeing — and they’re done with restrictive plans that work for two weeks then fall apart. I offer one-on-one coaching, personalized meal planning guidance, and habit-building strategies grounded in integrative nutrition and behavior change science. My approach treats food, stress, career, and relationships as connected pieces of the same puzzle. I became a health coach after watching my own health deteriorate under the pressure of a demanding corporate career — and discovering that real wellness isn’t about eating perfectly, it’s about building sustainable habits that support the life you actually want. I work with clients virtually from Austin, Texas.

Short Bio

Certified IIN Health Coach helping busy professional women ditch diet culture and build sustainable eating habits that fit their real lives. I combine nutrition guidance with behavior change coaching, because wellness isn’t just about food — it’s about the whole picture.

Micro Bio

Health Coach for professional women ready to eat well without dieting. Sustainable habits, real life.

Example 3
BCHN — Functional Nutrition
Long Bio

I’m a Board Certified Holistic Nutritionist (BCHN) through the NANP, with additional training in functional nutrition and clinical herbalism. I work with adults navigating complex health challenges — autoimmune conditions, hormonal imbalances, chronic fatigue, and metabolic concerns — who are looking for root-cause answers, not just symptom management. My services include comprehensive nutritional assessments, individualized therapeutic protocols, targeted supplementation plans, and food-as-medicine strategies. I use a functional nutrition framework: evaluating the whole person — digestion, detoxification, immune function, hormonal balance, and nutrient status — to create recommendations that address what’s actually driving the problem. My path to functional nutrition started with my own autoimmune diagnosis and the realization that conventional approaches weren’t asking the right questions. I serve clients locally in Denver and as distance clients nationwide.

Short Bio

Board Certified Holistic Nutritionist specializing in complex health challenges through functional nutrition. I help adults with autoimmune, hormonal, and metabolic concerns find root-cause answers through comprehensive assessments and individualized protocols.

Micro Bio

BCHN using functional nutrition to help adults with complex health challenges find root-cause answers.
